What is MyBus?

MyBus is a user-friendly online bus booking system developed using Django. It makes intercity travel easy by helping passengers search routes, check seat availability, and book tickets online with a few clicks.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, reliable, and convenient bus booking experience through a modern digital platform that makes transportation accessible for everyone.
